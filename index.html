Aleksandre Kartvelishvili

Georgian man who has changed the world



Mr. Kartveli with aircraft models of his designs

Here's a time line of Kartvelishvili:

  • 1896 Born in Georgia, Tbilisi

  • 1914 Graduates from school in Tbilisi

  • 1922 Finishes the Highest School Of Aviation in Paris

  • 1922 | 1927 Works at the Louis Bleriot companiy where he designs "Bernard" and "Ferbois" aricrafts

  • 1924 One of his aircraft establishes a world speed record

  • 1927 The American millionaire Charles Levine invites Kartvelishvili to New York, to join the Atlantic Aircraft Corporation. There he creates "Uncle Sam" the first aircraft which flew over the Atlantic Ocean

  • 1928 He joins a famous "Fokker American Company"

  • 1931 Kartveli co-founds "Seversky Aircraft Corporation" with Alexander de Seversky to create military aircrafts. Here Kartveli is a chief engineer

  • 1935 Kartveli's company produces P-35 which was the first millitary aircraft for USA

  • 1939 Seversky was removed as head of his co-founded company and the "Republic Aviation Company" was born

  • 1941 | 1945 During the World War II Republic Aviation produces more than 15000 P-47 aircafts which plays a big role during the war

  • 1946 Kartveli produces another millitary aircraft F-84 Thunderjet. A total of 10,000 of these new generation aircraft have been constructed

  • 1946 | 1974 Duaring this period of time Kartveli constructs a few more great aircrafts.

  • 1974 Alexander Kartveli died on July 20, 1974, at Huntington (Long Island) Hospital, New York, USA. He was 77 years old at that time and he was still warking at Republic Aviation Corporation.
